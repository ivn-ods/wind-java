About the project

wind.od.ua is a project for monitoring wind speed and direction online by weather stations at various points in the Odessa region. Although it was conceived for kiting, it is perfect for various sports where wind speed is important - for windsurfing, paragliding, parachuting, yachting, etc.

The wind speed measurement at the spot is carried out by self-developed solar-powered Internet weather stations with data transmission using the GSM network. The minimum, average, and maximum wind speed is calculated for 15 minutes at a 20-second time interval. Data is transmitted to the server via GPRS once every 5-15 minutes in normal mode depending of wind speed and solar energy and once every 1 hour at night.

The air temperature in the sun is also measured

At the moment, GSM weather stations are installed in Koblevo (all directions), Luzanovka (directions from S to E), Sergeevka (all directions).

Rules of use

When registering, a user can log in to the site from up to three different computers. For a successful login, browser support for cookies is required, incl. from a mobile phone.

When you first enter the site from a new computer, you must enter the number of this computer (1..3), login and password. Then the login will occur automatically.

If you want to change one of the three permanent computers, you must sign in to the new computer, and the old computer will be disabled. You can log in again on the old computer only the next day and the new computer will be disabled.

Data parsing is prohibited! Users using automatic html parsing to extract data and distribute it further will be banned immediately!
