Registration

Subscription to the anemometer is paid, and costs 600 UAH per year. If you agree, please fill in all fields (English letters and numbers are allowed). After clicking on the "Register" button, you will be redirected to the Privat LiqPay payment system, where from several payment methods you can choose the most convenient one. After receiving confirmation from LiqPay, you will receive full access to the site for 1 year. You can use other payment methods, contact by phone, specified in the section Contact
